Frequently Asked Questions

Why don’t I see real photos of each car on the website?

To protect privacy and keep the site fast, we display a representative image of each model. When you inquire, we send 10+ real photos of the exact unit within 2 hours via WhatsApp — plus a walk-around video and the full condition report.

Can I see the car before paying the deposit?

Yes. We send full photo galleries, video walk-arounds, auction inspection sheets and a detailed condition report before any payment. The KES 200,000 deposit only comes after you’ve seen everything and decided to proceed.

Is duty and tax (VAT) included in the price?

The displayed price is FOB Japan (port-to-port). Kenya import duty, VAT, IDF and clearance are not included. Use the Total Landed Cost calculator on any car page for a realistic estimate to Mombasa or Nairobi.

How long does shipping from Japan to Mombasa take?

RoRo shipping takes approximately 4–6 weeks from Japan to Mombasa Port. Container Share is similar but slightly faster on average and offers more protection for high-value vehicles.

Can you handle the clearance at Mombasa Port?

Yes. We work with bonded clearing agents at Mombasa Port and can deliver the car directly to your location in Nairobi, Kisumu, Eldoret or anywhere in Kenya.

Do you accept M-Pesa payments?

Yes. The KES 200,000 deposit can be paid via M-Pesa. The remaining CIF balance is typically paid via bank transfer (Wise, SWIFT) for security and tax-record purposes.

What documents do I need to import a car to Kenya?

We provide the full export package: Japanese export certificate, JEVIC pre-shipment inspection, bill of lading, commercial invoice and KEBS conformity certificate. You only need your KRA PIN and ID for clearance.
