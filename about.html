Built by someone who saw the problem first-hand

Quality Japanese cars, delivered to Kenya by a real team — in Japan and on the ground in Nairobi.

When the founder first visited Kenya, one thing was impossible to miss: Japanese cars everywhere. Toyotas, Hondas, Subarus, Nissans — they had clearly become the heart of how Kenyans move.

But spending more time on the ground revealed the other side. Many cars in the local market were old, tired, and quietly tampered with — odometers rolled back, conditions inflated, real history buried. Buyers were paying real money for vehicles that wouldn’t last. And we all know the saying: cheap ends up expensive.

That’s why Best Cars 254 was built. Our mission is simple: bring quality Japanese cars directly to the people of Kenya — with full transparency, real inspections, and a price that respects you. We’ve assembled a strong team in Japan and Kenya so every car that crosses the ocean is one we’d be proud to drive ourselves.

Better cars. Honest process. Real people you can call. That’s the bridge we built — because Kenyans deserve to drive something that lasts.

Our Certifications

QISJ — Quality Inspection Standard Japan

Every car we export gets an independent pre-shipment inspection by QISJ. The certificate documents condition, verified mileage, and any defects. You receive the QISJ certificate before paying the balance — so what you see is what you get.

JUMVEA — Japan Used Motor Vehicle Exporters Association

We work exclusively with JUMVEA-certified exporters — including major names like BE FORWARD and GOONET Exchange. JUMVEA membership requires a verifiable business address, registered license, and strict code-of-conduct compliance.

Your payment goes directly to the JUMVEA-certified exporter in Japan — never to a middleman. The exporter’s JUMVEA certificate can be shared with you before any payment is made. Just ask.
