How It Works · 6 Steps

From a Japan auction to your driveway in Kenya

Six steps. Honest documentation at every stage.

1

Tell us what you want.

Tap “Get Photos & Quote” or message our Sales Team on WhatsApp at +254 735 470450 / +254 734 010654. Share model, year range, max mileage, budget. No deposit, no commitment.

2

We send you real photos and details.

We pull actual units matching your request from current auction listings — exterior, interior, undercarriage, and the auction sheet. Before any money moves.

3

We confirm the price together.

Our Japan exporter provides the official pricing, and our team works alongside you to negotiate the best deal possible. Total landed cost to Mombasa: vehicle + Japan export + RoRo freight + QISJ inspection + insurance. No hidden margins. Auction grade sheet shared.

4

USD 2,000 refundable deposit.

Refundable until we win the auction. Bank transfer only — sent directly to our JUMVEA-certified exporter in Japan. No middlemen. Receipt issued. (Want to verify the exporter first? Ask for the JUMVEA certificate — we send it before you pay.)

5

We ship Japan → Mombasa.

Typical arrival is 45 to 60 days from purchase to Mombasa Port. We keep you updated throughout — and whenever new photos are available, we send them. You also receive the bill of lading, QISJ certificate, and commercial invoice.

6

You clear and drive home.

Our Kenya team coordinates with a bonded clearing agent at Mombasa. Pay the balance + duty. Delivery to Nairobi, Kisumu, Eldoret — anywhere in Kenya.

Our Certifications

QISJ — Quality Inspection Standard Japan

Every car we export gets an independent pre-shipment inspection by QISJ. The certificate documents condition, verified mileage, and any defects. You receive the QISJ certificate before paying the balance — so what you see is what you get.

JUMVEA — Japan Used Motor Vehicle Exporters Association

We work exclusively with JUMVEA-certified exporters — including major names like BE FORWARD and GOONET Exchange. JUMVEA membership requires a verifiable business address, registered license, and strict code-of-conduct compliance.

Your payment goes directly to the JUMVEA-certified exporter in Japan — never to a middleman. The exporter’s JUMVEA certificate can be shared with you before any payment is made. Just ask.
